matias and aurora
july / 2 / 2022 - tampere

When I met Matias and Aurora, I was struck by how effortlessly relaxed they were about the planning. It made perfect sense when I learned Matias works in event organization—and with their family's help, they created something truly special.

This was a wedding where everyone contributed their talents. Siblings and parents were involved in every detail: designated drivers, DJs, bakers—you name it, someone they loved was behind it.

This collaborative approach created something beautifully unique. Every detail reflected not just the couple, but the deep bonds they share with the people who matter most. It wasn't just a wedding—it was unmistakably their wedding.
